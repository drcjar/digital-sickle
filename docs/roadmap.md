# Roadmap

Aligned to the MoU timeline.

## Jun – Sept 2026 — Partnership development
- Secure the MoU between the Observatory (NHS Confederation) and the NHS acute trust.
- Publish this repository and project website; work in the open.
- Use this prototype to make the service tangible for the MoU and tender conversations.
- Prepare and issue the invitation to tender.

## Oct 2026 – May 2027 — Digital service build
- Appoint a delivery partner.
- Build the production service: NHS Care Identity (CIS2) SSO, managed cloud hosting,
  information governance (DPIA), NHS Notify for messaging, antivirus-scanned uploads,
  organisation-boundary RBAC, and integration/workflow optimisation with clinical systems.

## Jun 2027 – Sept 2028 — Pilot and iteration
- Private beta with selected patients at the partner trust.
- Evaluate impact on access to timely analgesia, patient experience, and accountability.
- Iterate; feed anonymised data into dashboards and research datasets.

## What production adds beyond this prototype
This prototype deliberately stops short of production concerns so the delivery partner owns
them. Production must add, at minimum:
- CIS2 / smartcard SSO for clinicians and assured patient identity.
- Information governance: DPIA, data protection by design, NHS-approved hosting.
- Real email verification and messaging (NHS Notify).
- Antivirus scanning of uploads and stricter content controls.
- Penetration testing and clinical safety (DCB0129/0160) assurance.
